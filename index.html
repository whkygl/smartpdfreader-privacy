Third-Party AI Services ç¬¬ä¸‰æ–¹ AI æœåŠ¡

SmartPDF Reader integrates with third-party AI services to provide intelligent document assistance. When you use these features:

Google Gemini API

  • PDF content and your questions are sent directly to Google's Gemini API
  • Images you upload for analysis are sent to Google's servers
  • Your API key is used for authentication
  • Data handling is governed by Google's Privacy Policy

Deepseek API

  • PDF content and your questions are sent directly to Deepseek's API
  • Your API key is used for authentication
  • Data handling is governed by Deepseek's Privacy Policy

Important: We do not have access to the data you send to these third-party services. Your conversations go directly from your device to their servers using your own API keys.
